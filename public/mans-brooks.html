MEN'S BROOKS GHOST 15


Men's BROOKS GHOST 15:

The same Ghost you love has been updated as the Men's Brooks Ghost 15; it's now lighter and more comfortable than ever. Thanks to a new midsole featuring DNA Loft v2 cushioning, your daily trainer weighs 9.8 oz: that's even less than the Ghost 14. An engineered air mesh upper is 3D printed to ensure perfection and breathability. Start building up your mileage because your Ghost 15 has High Performance Rubber in the heel for extra durable road protection.

RUNNING SHOE TECHNOLOGY:

  • Air Mesh Upper: Engineered double Jacquard mesh is lightweight and soft.
  • Structured Upper: Your 3D Fit Print adds structure to the breathable upper you love.
  • Comfort Collar: Experience plush details throughout your shoe with extra padding in the heel.
  • Lightweight Cushioning: A full length DNA Loft v2 midsole adds comfort and absorbs shock.
  • Smooth Transitions: A segmented crash pad allows for soft landings no matter how you run.
